Physics:
--------
This feature implements ice-coverage-dependent chemical desorption efficiency.

On bare silicate/graphite grains:
- Higher desorption efficiency due to direct surface interaction
- Calculated using Minissale et al. 2016 formalism

On ice-covered grains:
- Lower desorption efficiency (energy absorbed by ice matrix)
- Fredon+ 2021 / Furuya+ 2022 formalism
- OR simplified: 1/10 of bare grain efficiency (Minissale+ 2016)

The code interpolates linearly between these extremes based on ice thickness:
- 0 monolayers → bare grain efficiency
- 1+ monolayers → full ice coverage efficiency
- Intermediate → linear interpolation
